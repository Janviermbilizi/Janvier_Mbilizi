About Me

Janvier is from DR. Congo, took refuge in Burundi and is living in the United States permanently since April 2017. He was resettled in Virginia with his family then, moved to Denver, Colorado. From surviving persecution and family separation, the world has given him a chance to learn and speak from experience. If it does not kill you, it will make you stronger, a motto that has helped Janvier overcome his biggest obstacles. Despite all of his negative experiences, Janvier has a degree in Geology and mining from the University of Burundi and he speaks seven human languages. Janvier is a self-taught web-developer and has a passion for learning more computer programming languages. Today, he works full time as a Case Manager at Lutheran Family Services (Refugee and asylum program) in Denver, Colorado while attending a full stack web-development Bootcamp at University of Denver (DU). He strives to support refugee communities by providing the same services and opportunities he was given.
